Five additions layered onto the existing card without changing its shape, size, or hover behavior.

The five additions

  • Care-level badge — top-right. Colored pill: green (beginner), yellow (intermediate), orange (advanced), red (expert).
  • Taxon chip — bottom-left. Icon or short label showing what kind of critter/plant it is.
  • Advisory warning pill — top-left, only when hobbyistAdvisory is set. Red pill signaling "specialist-only" species.
  • Hover teaser — appears on hover, below the card. First sentence of summary.
  • Trade-name explainer — bottom-right, only when the card matched a search via alsoKnownAs. Chip like "also: red neon tetra".
