Four visually distinct pills for the four levels — same shape and position (top-left), same size (small pill), different color + icon + label:

  • Specialist — red, ⚠ — the 73-species majority. "You can do this, but it's demanding."
  • Restricted — dark gray, ⚖ — legal issue. "You may not be allowed to own this."
  • Public only — purple, ⚑ — impossible at home. "No home tank can hold this."
  • Pond only — blue, ↑ — belongs outdoors. "Great fish, wrong container."

The reason string (from hobbyistAdvisory.reason) could show as a tooltip on the pill, or in the hover teaser area, or on the detail page only. Not surfaced on the card by default in this mockup.
