WHY THIS IS TRUSTWORTHY
• The number is grounded in expert-curated fields (minVol + minGroupSize), not a guessed mass formula.
• A starter group of any species exactly equals its own min tank — the model sanity-checks itself.
• Every value is explainable in words: "6 tiger barbs use ~56 L of your 200 L."
• Species missing those fields fall back to adultSizeCm × diet-messiness, calibrated to the same scale.
• Shown as bands, not false precision — the goal is guidance, and it errs toward "don't overstock."
